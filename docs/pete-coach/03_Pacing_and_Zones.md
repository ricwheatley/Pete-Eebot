# Pacing and Zones

## Based on Current State

- Easy: conversational.
- Steady: purposeful but controlled.
- Tempo: comfortably hard.
- Interval: high intensity, limited dose.

## Marathon Target

- Sub-3 pace: about 4:15/km.
- Treat this as a long-term anchor, not current default training pace.

## Adjustment Rules

- HR drift up: reduce intensity.
- HRV down: shift toward easy running.
- Readiness amber: reduce quality or duration.
- Readiness red: no quality session.
- Underfueling plausible: fuel first, then reassess training load.
